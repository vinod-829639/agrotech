    About Agrotech

    Empowering Farmers with Intelligent Disease Detection

    At Agrotech, we're dedicated to transforming agriculture by providing farmers with cutting-edge solutions for early disease detection and targeted treatment. Our mission is to minimize crop loss, optimize resource utilization, and promote sustainable farming practices through intelligent technology.

    We understand the challenges farmers face in identifying and managing plant diseases. That's why we've developed a comprehensive platform that leverages advanced algorithms and remote sensing technologies to detect diseases in their earliest stages, allowing for timely intervention and effective treatment.

    Our Story

    Agrotech was founded in 2020 by Vinod Hosmani. Witnessing the devastating impact of crop diseases on local farms, they envisioned a solution that could combine their expertise to proactively protect crops and secure livelihoods.

    Starting with a pilot program in raichur specially in sirwar taluk, Agrotech quickly gained recognition for its accurate disease detection capabilities and the significant cost savings it provided to farmers. Since then, we've expanded our services to cover a wide range of crops and regions, partnering with leading agricultural research institutions and technology providers.

    Looking ahead, Agrotech is committed to pushing the boundaries of technology-driven agriculture. We're continuously giving tarining for field visitors and expanding our network of our company to provide farmers with the most comprehensive and effective solutions available. We envision a future where technology empowers farmers to grow healthy, abundant crops while minimizing their environmental impact.

    Our Guiding Principles

    Accuracy

    We are committed to providing the most accurate disease detection results possible, ensuring that farmers can make informed decisions based on reliable data.

    Innovation

    We continuously training our field visitors and development to stay at the forefront of technology and provide our customers with the latest advancements in disease detection.

    Sustainability

    We believe in promoting sustainable farming practices by helping farmers minimize pesticide use and optimize resource utilization, contributing to a healthier environment.

    Customer Success

    We are dedicated to the success of our customers, providing personalized support and tailored solutions to meet their specific needs and challenges.
